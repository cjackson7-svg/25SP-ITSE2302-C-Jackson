Section One: 7-Day Jumpstart

Kick off your journey with our 7-Day Jumpstart! Each day focuses on building small, healthy habits such as drinking more water, moving for 20 minutes, or swapping soda for a smoothie. The goal isn’t perfection; it’s progress. By the end of the week, you’ll feel more energized and motivated to take on the full challenge.

Section Two: 30-Day Challenge

The heart of the Fit Girls Challenge is our 30-Day program. You’ll follow a balanced routine of strength training, cardio, and recovery days! perfect for beginners or anyone looking to get back on track. Each week introduces new moves to keep it fun and push you a little further. Track your progress and celebrate the wins, big or small!

Section Three: Mind + Body Reset

Fitness isn’t just about workouts— . it’s about mindset. This section helps you reset with self-care routines, positive affirmations, and mindful practices to keep you balanced. Whether it’s journaling for 5 minutes, stretching before bed, or celebrating your progress, the Mind + Body Reset keeps your journey sustainable and enjoyable.
